PANEL BONDING / REPAIRING OF LED, LCD TELEVISION

The most encountered problems with an LCD/LED television is its panel. If you have encountered any problem in your television screen like unnecessary horizontal-vertical lines passing, or half display, then your television panel requires a repair. We also offer the bonding services of your LED/LCD(3D, 4K, Curved tv) television panel with 32- 80 inches of multi-brands like SAMSUNG, SONY, SHARP, PHILIPS, LLOYD, TOSHIBA, HAIER, PANASONIC etc. We have high-end panel bonding machines and experienced staff to perform panel bonding services.

At television care, the fault diagnosis is accurate and if it comes out to be with the panel of the television, then extra care must be given at the time of service. Some of the panel issues that mostly occurs almost in all televisions are horizontal blue lines, the presence of white lines raise the need for panel repair. Get the best panel repair/bonding services for your television and restore your entertainment. You can avail our service for your television of any brand and we will ensure that the work is completed as soon as possible. In the case of panel replacement, we ensure the honest cost of the estimate and will try to make it a cost-effective one also.
